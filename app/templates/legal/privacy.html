This Privacy Policy explains how TikTok Creator ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our application.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session
  • To store and serve your uploaded or generated media
  • To publish content to TikTok on your behalf using your provided access token
  • To show you a history of your posts
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing

3. TikTok API Data

When you connect your TikTok account, we store your TikTok access token to make API calls on your behalf. We only request the minimum scopes required:

  • video.publish — to publish videos to TikTok
  • video.upload — to upload video files
  • user.info.basic — to identify your TikTok account

We do not access your TikTok feed, followers, messages, or any data beyond what is listed above. Your TikTok token is used solely to post content you explicitly choose to publish through this App.

4. Third-Party Services

The App may send data to the following third-party services to function:

  • Hugging Face (huggingface.co) — your text prompts are sent to their Inference API to generate images
  • Replicate (replicate.com) — your text prompts are sent to generate AI videos
  • TikTok (tiktok.com) — your media and captions are sent to TikTok's Content Posting API
  • Railway (railway.app) — our hosting provider where your data is stored

5. Data Retention

Your data is retained as long as your account is active. You can delete individual media files from your library at any time. To request full account deletion, contact us at the email below.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data including bcrypt password hashing, HTTPS in transit, and HTTP-only cookies for session tokens. No system is 100% secure; use a strong unique password.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for TikTok integration at any time (by removing your token)

8. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes.
